Site Purpose

The purpose of the website is to provide up to date weather to the residents of Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven, Idaho. The home page will have an at glance look at the current weather as well as the current conditions in each town. This will help residents who need to know the weather to determine how they want to plan their day. There will be pages dedicated to each town as well as two additional pages for storm center and gallery. The goal is to provide as accurate a weather reading for each town to help its residents know what to expect as far as weather to enjoy their day.

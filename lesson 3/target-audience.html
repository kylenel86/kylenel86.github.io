Target Audience

The target audience is meant for teens and adults in south eastern Idaho who are looking for accurate weather updates, especially citizens who live in Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven. It is meant for both males and females with no income restrictions. The site will be pleasing and functional.

Personas

Gender: Male
Age: 30-65
Income: $40-80k/year
Occupation: Teacher
Education: Bachelor's or Master’s Degree
Experience with the topic: Checks the weather 2x/day
Family status: Single with no children
Where, when and how the visitor will be accessing the site: From home, every morning/night from a mobile device
Purpose for checking weather: Dress appropriately for job, best time to leave to commute to work, and for school closures in winter

Gender: Female
Age: 20-30
Income: $0/year
Education: High School, AA or Bachelor's Degree
Occupation: Stay at Home Mom
Experience with the topic: Checks the weather daily
Family status: Married with 3 children
Where, when and how the visitor will be accessing the site: From home on phone, tablet or laptop
Purpose for checking weather: Plan best leave time for dropping off children, what activiteis can be done inside or outside for little children


Scenarios

Scenario 1

A 35-year-old male who is a surgeon is about to spend a 10-hour day the next day at the hospital in Preston is wanting to check the weather. It is fall had he wants to know if it will possibly rain. He is tired and is already in bed so wants to look online on his phone to see how he should plan on dressing and if he needs to bring an umbrella to work.

Scenario 2

A 16-year-old female has been asked out on her first date. She is very excited to spend an evening going to dinner and miniature golfing. She is watching a movie on her tablet and wants to check what the weather is like so she can decide what is best to wear if she can go without a jacket or not.

Scenario 3

A 24-year old male college student is hoping to take a break on the weekend to go hiking in Soda Springs. He is finishing up an assignment on his laptop and goes to the website to check and make sure it will be a clear day to make for a smoother hike. It will be and he decides that it would be more fun to include friends in his plans.

Scenario 4

It is Thanksgiving week and a 70-year-old grandmother is looking forward to all her children coming in for the holiday. Due to the time of year it could potentially snow. She wants to check her desktop computer to see if that will be the case or not. She would like to inform her children what to expect and if they may even need to spend the night afterwards if it is that bad.

Scenario 5

A 33-year old single female is going to her college roommates wedding. She lives out of state and has no idea what to expect the weather will be. Her friend suggests the site so she takes out her cell phone and looks at the website so she can pack her luggage accordingly.

Target Audience

Personas

Gender: Male
Age: 30-65
Income: $40-80k/year
Occupation: Teacher
Education: Bachelor's or Master’s Degree
Experience with the topic: Checks the weather 2x/day
Family status: Single with no children
Where, when and how the visitor will be accessing the site: From home, every morning/night from a mobile device
Purpose for checking weather: Dress appropriately for job, best time to leave to commute to work, and for school closures in winter

Gender: Female
Age: 20-30
Income: $0/year
Education: High School, AA or Bachelor's Degree
Occupation: Stay at Home Mom
Experience with the topic: Checks the weather daily
Family status: Married with 3 children
Where, when and how the visitor will be accessing the site: From home on phone, tablet or laptop
Purpose for checking weather: Plan best leave time for dropping off children, what activiteis can be done inside or outside for little children


Scenarios

  • Scenario 1

    • A 35 year old male

  • Scenario 2

    • A 25 year old married woman arrives at the website through her phone because she is planning an outside birthday party for her husband. She searches through the available locations until she finds her area and click on it to see an extended 10 day forcast, since his birthday is in a week. She puruses the forcasted weather and sees that the weather is supposed to be bad. So she bookmarks the site to keep tabs on the weather if it changes. She returns daily to check and see if the weather changes so she can throw an outside Birthady party.

  • Scenario 3

    • A 30 year old mother wakes up early to check the weather. It is December and she knows that it is cold outside so she is checking the temperature to see if it is cold enough for the schools to be canceled so she can know if she has to get her kids up or not. She notices that on the front page it is giving the current temperature and there are recent news articles stating that schools will be canceled in teh area because fo a severe wind chill. She is happy because she got her answers and then goes and makes herself some hot cocoa to warm up.

  • Scenario 4

    • A 40 year old father is checking the weather on his phone just before he goes to bed because he wants to take his son to a football game the next day but he wants to know if it will be cold or not. He searches for his town and finds the extended forcast. He realizes that the weather will cold but decent. So he decides to wake his son up in the morning and tell him the good news. The father bookmarks the site in his phone for future reference.

  • Scenario 5

    • A 53 year old woman is looking up the weather to be able to plan her family's reunion in the next week. She find her city on the home page and goes to the weather page for the extended forcast. There she notices that the weather in supposed to be nice so she goes ahead with her plans for the reunion. But she bookmarks the page to be able check every day just in case the weather changes. She is happy that she will be able to spend the tiem with her family outside.
